* A dark spirit/alien entity has overcome the world and blocks the sun out with a permanent eclipse. As such, unlight areas have a twilight hue
* All creatures at the surface are possessed by this entity and become aggressive. These creatures are marked by red eyes or a red aura 
* To correct the world, the players must destroy the moon to end the eclipse
* The players are civilians from a hub of survivors in a city that have discovered how to end the apocalypse
